Young Whale Helps Its Mum

24-10-2022 出典
A humpback whale with her calf was filmed east of Brisbane where the mother was stranded in shallow waters. The calf appeared to nudge its mother to help dislodge her to safety. Fortunately, the whale became free after 40 minutes and could be seen swimming off with her calf.

単語
  • humpback whale (ザトウクジラ)
  • calf (the baby of an animal such as a whale)
  • stranded (unable to leave)
  • nudge (push, bump against)
  • dislodge (move)

World’s first intestine transplant

20-10-2022 出典
A one-year-old Spanish girl named Emma became the first intestine transplant recipient. When she was just one month old, doctors diagnosed Emma with intestinal failure, and her health rapidly deteriorated because her intestine was too short. Emma’s disease stopped her intestine’s job to absorb the energy needed to live. The most common cause of intestinal failure in children is a short intestine. Emma received her new intestine, liver, stomach, spleen, and pancreas from a donor who died of heart failure in Madrid’s La Paz hospital. Spain is a global leader in organ transplants with over 102 performed in 2021. According to Spanish health ministry data, only the US surpasses this rate.

単語
  • intestine (腸)
  • deteriorate (to become worse)
  • spleen (脾臓; a small organ above the stomach that cleans the blood)
  • pancreas (膵臓; an organ that controls the amount of sugar in blood, and it helps digest food)
